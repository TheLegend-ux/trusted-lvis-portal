⚠️ Critical Skill

Avoiding Phishing in Schools

Education is the #1 target for credential harvesting.

🚨 Recent Threat

Fake "IT Admin" emails requesting password updates for LMS accounts are on the rise.

🔎 Verify the Sender

Hover over the sender name to reveal the real email address.

🖱️ Hover Before Clicking

Check the true destination URL in the bottom of your browser.

⏰ Urgency is a Red Flag

Phishers use "Immediate Action Required" lines to induce panic.

📧 Reporting Protocol

Use the "Report Phishing" button to alert the security team.

1. The 2FA Foundation

What is 2FA?

Think of it as a double-locked door. Even if someone finds your password, they still need a special token to enter.

Something you know (Password)
Something you have (Authenticator App)
Attacks Blocked

2FA stops almost all automated account takeovers.

3. How to Secure Your Email

Email is the primary target for educational phishing.

01
Check "Third-Party Apps"

Revoke permissions in your Google Accountor Microsoft Account.

02
Enable Phishing Protection

Ensure "Safe Links" and "Safe Attachments" are enabled if your institution provides them.

03
The "hover" test

Never click a link without hovering first to verify the true destination URL.
